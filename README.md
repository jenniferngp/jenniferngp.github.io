# Hello! I'm Jennifer

I am a Bioinformatics PhD student studying human genetics and genomics. I leverage large multi-omics datasets generated from iPSCs and derived tissues to functionally characterize the regulatory mechanisms of genetic variants associated with human complex traits and diseases. Formerly a wet-lab scientist, I transitioned into Bioinformatics because I was fascinated by the power of computational methods to analyze and interpret vast amounts of biological data in a short period of time. 

This websites serves as my personal digtal repository for my notes and tutorials. I have curated this space not just for my own reference but with the hope that others may find it beneficial. .
