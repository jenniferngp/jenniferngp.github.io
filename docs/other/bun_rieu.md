Bun rieu, also known as tomato noodle soup

Ingredients:

- 4 chicken drumsticks
- 6 cups of water
- 2 lemongrass stalks (cut in half, peeled, and beatened)
- 1 tbsp Instant Tom Yum sauce
- 2 roma tomatoes
- 1/8 tsp MSG
- 3 tsp sugar
- 3 tsp salt
- Tofu cubes (as many as you want; fried or not, both are delicious)

Steps:

1. Boil water with chicken drumsticks, MSG, sugar, and salt
2. After water boils, add lemongrass. 
3. Cover pot with lid and simmer for 12 min
4. Turn off heat. Place pot to the side and let sit for ~12 min.
5. Add tomatos, tofu, and tom yum sauce. Mix. 
6. Serve with ~1/4 tbsp shrimp paste!
