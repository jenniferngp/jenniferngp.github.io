# Banana Coconut Pudding (Che Chuoi)

### Ingredients:

- 1 pound cubed taro
- 0.5 pound cubed cassava (~$2.5/pound at grocery store, pre-peeled)
- 1 packet vanilla sugar
- 5 tbsp sugar
- half of 1/3 cup peeled mung bean (yellow)
- half of 1/3 cup dried tapioca pearl (pink and green rectangle)
- half of 1/3 cup tapioca pearl
- 5 cups water
- 1 cup coconut cream
- 0.5 tsp salt

### Steps:

1. Wash taro and cassava. Cut into cubes. 
2. Boil 4 cups of water. Cook cassava in boiling water for 8 minutes. Drain.
3. Wash dried tapioca pearl and mung bean. 
4. Boil 5 cups of water. Add dried tapioca pearl and mung bean. Simmer for 10 minutes. 
5. Add washed tapioca pearl and simmer for 15 more minutes
6. Add taro and simmer for 8 minutes
7. Add sugar, coconut cream, vanilla sugar, and salt
8. Serve hot or cold! Delicious at both temps!
