# Chicken Pho (Pho Ga)

### Ingredients:

- 4 chicken drum sticks
- 6 cups water
- 1.8 tsp MSG
- 3 tsp sugar
- 3 tsp salt
- 5 cloves
- 1 tsp coriander
- 1 tsp fennel seeds
- 1 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick

### Steps :

1. Boil 4 chicken drum sticks in 6 cups of water with 3 tsp sugar and 3 tsp salt
2. Once it starts boiling, add clove, coriander, fennel seeds, star anise, and cinnamon
3. Cover and simmer for 12 min
4. Turn off heat. Move the pot to the side. Keep covered and "cook" for another 12 min
5. Serve with cilantro, slices of red onions, hoisin sauce, and siracha
