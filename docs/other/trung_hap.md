# Steamy Egg Cake (Trung Hap)

### Ingredients:

- 1 pound ground meat (pre-seasoned with sugar, MSG, and pepper)
- 5 whole eggs
- 1 bunch of bean thread noodles
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/8 tsp MSG 
- Pepper (trust your gut)
- 1 small onion
- 2.5 tbsp fish sauce
- 0.25 tbsp chopped thai chilli peppers

### Steps :

1. Soak noodles in boiling water for 15 min
2. Mix all ingredients
3. Steam for 30 min

Notes:

- Most popular bean thread noodles are the ones in a neon pink package
- Use glassware to steam the egg cake
