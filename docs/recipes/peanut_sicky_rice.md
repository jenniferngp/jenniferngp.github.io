# Peanut Coconut Sticky Rice (Xoi Dau)

### Ingredients:

- 1 lb peanuts (double sticky rice, equiv to 24oz)
- 4 cups water
- 1tsp salt
- 12 oz sticky rice 
- 8 oz water 
- 0.5tsp salt (equiv to 1 cup water)

### Steps :

1. Wash peanuts and cook in 4 cups of water and 1 tsp salt in an instant pot using the "Beans" function (35-40min)
2. Natural release the pressure and drain the peanuts (DO NOT WASH PEANUTS)
3. Wash and cook 12 oz of sticky rice in 8 oz water with peanuts and 0.5tsp salt for 10 min
4. Natural release for 2 min and immediate release pressure using chopsticks
5. Mix. Place lid back on and let it "cook" for another 5 min
6. Mix. Add 1 cup of coconut flakes
7. Store in a container and cover so the rice would not dry out
