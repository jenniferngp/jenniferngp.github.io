This protocol is for making 3x600ul of virus

Day 0

1. Seed 293T cells at 2x10^6 cells/well in 6-well plate (2ml media/well)
2. Prepare diluted plasmids at 100ng/ul

Day 1

1. Prepare eppendorfs for Tube A and Tube B 
2. To Tube A, add OptiMEM and Lipofectamine according to the instructions
* Total volumn = 500 ul
3. To Tube B, add OptiMem, packaging, and p3000 according to the instructions
* If you're doing more than one batch of virsus, I would create master mixes of both A and B and aliquot to their tubes. 
* Total volumn = 500 ul
4. To Tube A, add 1.4ug DNA
5. To Tube B, add Tube A mixture, dropwise. Gently mix well. 
* Total volumn = now 1ml
* Add plasmid to lipofectamine, not the other way around!
6. Incubate A+B mixture at RT, 15 min
7. Meanwhile, remove 1ml of media from each well, leaving only 1 ml of media left
8. Add 1ml of A+B mixture to well, dropwise. 293T detaches easily so need to do very gently
9. Incubate cells at 37C, 6h
10. Remove old media and add 2ml fresh media (DMEM). Add media very slowly to not detach the cells
11. Incubate cells at 37C, 48h

Day 4

1. Collect media into 15ml conical vials
2. Spin down to bring cells to bottom
3. Aliquot 600ul supernatant to 1.7ml eppendorfs
4. Concentrate if needed
5. Store viruses at -80C

* Sanity check: check each well under microscope for fluorescence in 293T
